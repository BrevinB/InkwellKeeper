# Inkwell Keeper - Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** [Current Date]

## Introduction

Inkwell Keeper ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and shared when you use our iOS application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Card Collection Data**: When you add cards to your collection, wishlist, or decks, this information is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework.
- **Camera Usage**: The app uses your device camera exclusively for scanning trading cards. Images are processed locally and are not stored, uploaded, or shared.

### Information Automatically Collected
- **No Personal Information**: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information.
- **No Analytics**: We do not use third-party analytics services.
- **No Tracking**: We do not track your behavior across other apps or websites.

## How We Use Your Information

All data collected by Inkwell Keeper is used solely for:
- Displaying your card collection
- Managing your wishlist
- Building and managing decks
- Calculating collection statistics
- Providing price estimates

## Data Storage

- **Local Storage Only**: All your collection data is stored exclusively on your device using SwiftData.
- **No Cloud Sync**: Currently, your data is not synced to iCloud or any other cloud service.
- **Your Control**: You can delete all app data at any time by deleting the app from your device.

## Third-Party Services

### Content Delivery
- **Lorcana Card Images**: Card images are loaded from the official Lorcana content delivery network (CDN) for display purposes only.

### Affiliate Links
- **Purchase Links**: The app includes affiliate links to TCGPlayer and eBay to help users purchase cards. When you click these links:
  - You are directed to third-party websites (TCGPlayer.com or eBay.com)
  - These sites have their own privacy policies
  - We may earn a commission from purchases (at no extra cost to you)
  - We do not receive any personal information about your purchases

### Price Information
- **Price Estimates**: Card price estimates are calculated locally using algorithms. In some cases, the app may query eBay's API for pricing information, but no personal data is transmitted.

## Children's Privacy

Inkwell Keeper does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app is rated 4+ and is safe for all ages.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access all data stored by the app (it's all on your device)
- Delete your data at any time (by deleting the app)
- Opt out of affiliate links (by not clicking them)

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

## Data Security

- All data is stored locally on your device
- No data is transmitted to external servers (except for optional price queries and affiliate links)
- Your device's built-in security features protect your data

## Camera Permissions

The app requests camera access to:
- Scan trading cards using optical character recognition (OCR)
- Automatically identify cards from your camera feed

Camera access is:
- **Optional**: You can use the app without granting camera access
- **Not Stored**: No photos or videos are saved
- **Local Processing**: All image processing happens on your device
