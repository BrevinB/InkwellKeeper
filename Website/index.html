Frequently asked questions

What is the best app to track a Disney Lorcana collection?

Ink Well Keeper is a free iPhone and iPad app built specifically for tracking Disney Lorcana collections. It combines batch card scanning, set completion tracking across every set plus promos, foil and Enchanted variant tracking, live market prices, and deck building in one native app — no spreadsheets or manual data entry required. Learn more

Can I scan Lorcana cards with my iPhone?

Yes. Ink Well Keeper's scanner recognizes any Lorcana card through your iPhone camera and adds it to your collection automatically. Multi-scan mode lets you scan a whole stack of cards in one session — point, scan, review, and add everything at once. Learn more

How much are my Lorcana cards worth?

Card values depend on rarity, variant, and demand — Enchanted and Legendary cards from popular characters are worth the most. Ink Well Keeper shows live market pricing for every card you own and totals your entire collection's value automatically, so you always know what your binder is worth. Learn more

How many cards are in a Lorcana deck?

A legal Disney Lorcana deck needs at least 60 cards, at most 4 copies of any card with the same full name, and cards from no more than 2 ink colors. Ink Well Keeper validates all of these rules automatically for Core Constructed, Infinity Constructed, and Triple Deck formats. Learn more

Can I import my Dreamborn collection?

Yes. Export your collection from dreamborn.ink as a CSV file, then choose Dreamborn CSV in Ink Well Keeper's Bulk Import screen. Your cards import in seconds with live progress. You can also paste a plain text card list. Learn more

What are Enchanted Lorcana cards?

Enchanted cards are rare alternate-art versions of existing cards with full-art treatment and special foiling — the chase cards of each set. Ink Well Keeper tracks Enchanted, Epic, Iconic, foil, and promo variants separately so your rarest pulls are counted correctly in your collection value. Learn more

Which Lorcana sets does Ink Well Keeper support?

Every Disney Lorcana set from The First Chapter onward, plus promo cards — with new sets added for launch day. Each set has a visual completion bar and missing-card filters. Learn more

Is Ink Well Keeper free?

Yes — downloading, scanning, collection tracking, set completion, pricing, and deck building are free with no ads. Optional Pro features add AI-powered deck completion and a rules assistant. Your collection data stays on your device. Download free
