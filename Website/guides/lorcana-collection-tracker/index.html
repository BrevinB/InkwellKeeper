Collecting Guide

The Best Way to Track Your Lorcana Collection

Once your Disney Lorcana collection passes a few hundred cards, "I'll remember what I have" stops working. Here's how collectors actually track their cards in 2026 — and how to get set up in a single evening.

Why track your collection at all?

Three things get dramatically easier with a real collection tracker:

  • Set completion. You know exactly which cards you're missing from each set, so you never buy singles you already own.
  • Value. You can see what your collection is worth — essential for insurance, trades, and knowing when a pull is a big deal.
  • Deck building. You can check in seconds whether you own the cards a deck list calls for.

Your options compared

A spreadsheet

Free and flexible, but every card is manual data entry, prices go stale immediately, and variants (foil, Enchanted, promo) turn your columns into chaos. Fine for 50 cards; painful for 500.

Websites like dreamborn.ink

Dreamborn is a great web tool for deck lists and playset tracking, but there's no card scanning — you add cards by searching for them one at a time — and it lives in a browser tab, not in your pocket at the card shop.

The official Companion app

Ravensburger's app has the full card catalog and a lore counter, but its collection features are basic: no scanning, no market prices, and no collection value.

A dedicated tracker app with scanning

A native iPhone app can use your camera to recognize cards, pull live prices, and keep everything offline on your device. That's the gap Ink Well Keeper was built to fill — it combines the scanner, the set checklist, the price guide, and the deck builder in one free app.

Set up your tracker in one evening

  1. Download Ink Well Keeper free from the App Store.
  2. Scan your binder. Open the scanner and work through your cards — multi-scan mode recognizes card after card without stopping. A 500-card collection is typically done in one sitting. See the scanning guide.
  3. Import anything you've already logged. If you've been using dreamborn.ink or a spreadsheet, import it via CSV or a pasted text list instead of re-entering it.
  4. Mark your variants. Foils, Enchanteds, and promos are tracked separately, so your collection value reflects what you actually own.
  5. Check your set progress. Every set gets a completion bar and a missing-cards filter — your new wishlist writes itself.
