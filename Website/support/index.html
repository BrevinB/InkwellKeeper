Support

Questions, bugs, or feature requests? Email support@inkwellkeeper.app — it goes straight to the developer, and I usually reply within a day.

My card won't scan

Make sure the card fills the frame in good light, without heavy glare on foils. You can always add any card manually from its set, or search by collector number (for example "Set 13 105").

How do prices work?

Prices come from public market data and are estimates for informational purposes. Cards without reliable data show "—" instead of a guess.

How do I import my existing collection?

Settings → Import supports Dreamborn CSV exports and plain card lists.

What does Pro include?

Ink Well Keeper Pro unlocks the AI Deck Builder and the Rules Assistant. Everything else — scanning, tracking, prices, sets, decks, sharing — is free, and always will be.

Is my collection backed up?

Your data is stored on your device, and syncs privately through your own iCloud account when sync is enabled.
